Typography direction

Headline — Inter Black 900, -0.04em tracking, tight leading 0.92
Product names — Inter Bold 700, normal tracking
Body / descriptions — Inter Regular 400, 1.5 line height
Labels / tags — Inter Medium 500, 0.1em letter spacing, uppercase

Key design notes for your designer

The falling image is the whole concept
It reads as "in freefall but relaxed" — that's the brand vibe. Someone who's comfortable in chaos. Place it large on the right of the hero, overlapping the headline. The arms-out pose naturally frames the text on the left. Remove the white background first at remove.bg
The red in the flannel = brand accent
The red from the shirt (#CC3333 approx) becomes the CTA colour and accent throughout. This ties the person to the brand visually — the shirt colour IS the button colour. Everything clicks.
Dark navy, not pure black
Pure black looks harsh. The #080814 dark navy has a slight blue tint — it matches the purple LED energy from the bedroom photo and makes the page feel more like a creative space than a generic dark site.
The "2M" social proof floats over the figure
Small floating card near the falling image showing "2M views" — builds instant credibility. Position it top-right of the figure so it reads while the eye is still on the image.
